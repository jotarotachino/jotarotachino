# Jotaro Tachino, MD, PhD

Emergency and critical care physician-scientist based in Japan.

I am interested in advancing **precision trauma care** by integrating trauma phenotyping, prognostic modeling, treatment-effect heterogeneity analysis, causal inference, and trauma proteomics.

My current research focuses on translating data-driven trauma phenotypes into clinically interpretable tools for risk visualization, treatment-effect evaluation, and collaborative trauma research.

## Featured project

### [Trauma-Vis](https://github.com/jotarotachino/Trauma-Vis)

**A web-based trauma phenotype visualization platform for precision trauma care.**

Trauma-Vis enables real-time visualization of trauma phenotypes and integrates conventional trauma prognostic models including ISS, RTS, TRISS, rSIG, and phenotype-augmented prognostic modeling.

- Live app: [Trauma-Vis Shiny App](https://jotarotachino.shinyapps.io/TraumaApp250305/)
- Repository: [jotarotachino/Trauma-Vis](https://github.com/jotarotachino/Trauma-Vis)
